About

Hello all, welcome to my site!

My name is Johan, and this website is dedicated to my love for homebrewing. Like so many others, the pandemic restrictions made me seek out new past-time activities that could be done from the comforts of my home. I have always had a strong passion for beer, but it was not until recently I actually took a shot at brewing my own! Needless to say I enjoy the process thoroughly, otherwise this website would not exist.

This website is in its essence an overview of my home brew. I hope you all will enjoy it, cheers!
